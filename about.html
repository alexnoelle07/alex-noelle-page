About me

Front-end developer from San Francisco

I am a native San Franciscan currently working as a preschool teacher at Genentech's 2nd Generation. I have my B.S. in Early Childhood Development from CSU East Bay. I have spent the past 10 years working with children and being a part of their growth and development.

With the pandemic's affect on schools and education, I was able to see a first-hand experience what distance learning looked like from the perspective of a child within the early-age range. I saw this as an opportunity to seek out a program that would teach me what I needed to help younger children successfully learn core concepts. I was able to find SheCodes and start my coding journey!

My other hobbies include photography. I mostly take family portraits but I have been doing more projects such as weddings and company photos. I love creative outlets and expressing that through many forms. With developers continuing to be in demand, I found it the perfect way to make a career change while still utilizing having a creative perspective on future projects.

So far I have built some basic web pages and a weather app. I have been enjoying every part of this learning experience and I encourage anyone who is thinking about joining this community to do so! I can't wait to see what I will create next with SheCodes!
